Built by people who never left the terrace

NARA'S WORLD started as a set of notes for friends who kept asking "what does that even mean?" during matches. It grew into a reference for anyone who wants to actually understand the game -- not just watch it.

We keep three things running side by side: a plain-language glossary of football terms, a growing directory of clubs, and short background profiles on players -- their roots, their rise, and their style of play.
